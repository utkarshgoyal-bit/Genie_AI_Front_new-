## üìß How to Appeal a Suspension

### Where to Submit

1. Go to: <https://play.google.com/console>
2. Navigate to your app
3. Click on "Policy Status" or the suspension notice
4. Click "Appeal" or "Submit Appeal"
5. Paste your customized appeal letter below

### Response Time

- Initial response: 24-72 hours
- Resolution: 3-7 days (sometimes longer)
